Coclo Landing Page — Demo Walkthrough

Walkthrough of the final landing page I designed and built for Coclo, a startup.

This is a demo. Coclo is currently in the launching process as of March 2026.

Overview

I used HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to design a landing page for the startup Coclo. Coclo stands for “college closet”; it’s a startup that helps make buying and selling clothes in college easy and accessible.

Process & approach

I focused on choosing design options and carefully crafting the experience around who the customer is and the needs of the company, so the landing page speaks directly to college students and supports Coclo’s mission.
