About

Hello! I'm a Master in Management Candidate at Chicago Booth School of Business. I have a BA in Media Arts & Design from the University of Chicago where I established the foundation for my interests; they sit at the intersection of marketing, design, data, and technology.

Through internships and project-based work, I’ve supported marketing operations, business development, and audience growth for both early-stage and established organizations. I’m interested in how creative ideas move from concept to execution, and my work focuses on the structure and strategy that help ideas take shape and reach real people. I’m drawn to environments where clarity allows creativity to grow!
